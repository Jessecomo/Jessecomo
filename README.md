### About me

I work at the intersection of AI quality and automation. At Stellar AI I evaluate model outputs, define what "good" looks like for human-in-the-loop systems, and redesign annotation guidelines to cut rework. On my own time I build the other side of that equation: full-stack apps that use LLMs as a tool.

Before this I spent six years running LightExcel, a North American custom vehicle-lighting business I founded and grew solo. That's where I learned to automate operations, make decisions from data, and ship without a safety net.

- 🔭 Currently building **NixFeed**, a full-stack AI newsletter-intelligence platform
- 🌱 Focused on AI quality, automation, and applied LLM work
- 📍 Based in Oshawa, ON
- 💬 Ask me about LLM evaluation, prompt design, or going from idea to production solo

### What I've built

**[NixFeed](https://github.com/jessecomo/nixfeed-overview) · AI Newsletter Intelligence Platform** — [live at nixfeed.app](https://nixfeed.app)
Reads across your newsletter subscriptions, strips ads and filler, and synthesizes one daily briefing that surfaces what actually matters. Solo-built full stack: inbound email webhooks, a Google Gemini pipeline, Stripe billing, and a Supabase data layer.
`React 19` · `TypeScript` · `Express.js` · `Supabase` · `Google Gemini` · `Stripe`

**[CSV AI Assistant](https://github.com/jessecomo/CSV-AI-Assistant) · Natural Language Data Analysis**
A chatbot that lets you ask plain-English questions about CSV data. Explains structure, detects patterns, generates sample questions, and keeps a memory buffer for coherent multi-turn conversations.
`Python` · `GPT-4o` · `LangChain`
